Encamera is a privacy-focused camera and photo vault app that you can actually trust. With a minimalist and intuitive interface, Encamera stands out from other photo lockers and photo vaults because of its well designed interface and exceptional privacy features.

Encamera secures personal photos and videos by encrypting every photo you take, storing them on your device or on your personal iCloud. No third-party storage means YOU are in control of your data - no need to trust anyone else’s servers and security. Lock intruders out with Face ID, PIN protection, and incredibly strong encryption. With Encamera, you can control where and how your secure data is stored.

Use Encamera for:
• Keeping special photos hidden from your gallery
• Storing documents and other personal data
• Everyday media protection
